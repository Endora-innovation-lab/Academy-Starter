## 📘 Academy Starter

**Academy Starter** is a simple and efficient platform to manage student attendance, fees, and batches for academies. Built for practical daily use.

---

## 🚀 Overview

Academy Starter is an early-stage application designed to simplify day-to-day operations for small academies and training institutes. It helps manage student data, attendance tracking, fee records, and batch organization in a structured and easy-to-use manner.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This project is currently in its **V1 (Prototype) stage**, created for testing and validation.

---

## ✨ Features (V1)

* **📋 Student Management** – Maintain a centralized record of all students.
* **📅 Attendance Tracking** – Easily log and monitor daily presence.
* **💰 Fee Management** – Track payments and outstanding dues.
* **🧑‍🏫 Batch Organization** – Group students by timing or subjects.
* **📱 Simple Interface** – A clean, mobile-friendly design for fast entry.
* **🌐 Web-Based Access** – Accessible from any browser.
